corrente

Sharing cultural benefits obtained
from a riparian ecosystem under
restoration

 
 
 

Corrente is a collective narration of the river Talfer/Talvera, where the voice of locals of Bozen/Bolzano intertwine with the sounds of the stream. Put on your headphones and click on the balloon to immerse yourself in these waters.

Why


Corrente is the result of a critical inquiry on the adequacy of one’s consumption, the energy it demands, and the needs it meets within a framework of increasing demand on renewable energy sources, namely hydropower production, in the alpine context of Bozen/Bolzano in SouthTyrol.

In turn, it exposes the way design can get intertwined in between local efforts to take care of rivers and promote the benefits they provide for local communities.

Value


This narration renders tangible aesthetic, spiritual and therapeutic benefits obtained from the River Talfer/Talvera, as it crosses Bolzano/Bozen; for locals to better perceive and give value to beneficial relations with a river that are not shaped by the industrial abstraction of water, and are rather based on reciprocal care.

This is based on an ecological framework where rivers are understood beyond the resources, or provisioning services they provide.

A balloon

The design artifact

Balloons are playful objects, that in this project, were used as metaphores of expandable human bladders, which receive and release water.

In short, a big transparent balloon was used as an artifact to collect and then spread in public places, the watery voice of river lovers and what their relation with the Talfer/Talvera entails.
