Hi, my name is Oscar Mauricio Rueda, and Corrente is the result of my path through the Master of Arts in Eco-Social Design at the Free University of Bolzano/Bozen. The project started as a small but hopeful effort to break up paradigmatic barriers between culture and nature, which turned out in a collective narration of the River Talfer/Talvera in Bozen/Bolzano (IT).
